Data Classification System

What is LIVE/Fresh?

Our LIVE/Fresh tier covers intelligence captured between one hour and twenty-four hours before it reaches you.

What is NonLive?

NonLive material is anything older than twenty-four hours; the archive runs uninterrupted back to 2012.

What is Basic Format?

Basic Format equals login sets taken from devices: email, username, phone, and password combinations packaged for copy-paste use.

It is a clean, simple option for clients who only need straightforward account access.

What is Full Format?

Full Format contains the entire host footprint: browser history, cookies, tokens, clipboard artifacts, identity records, device data, hardware specs, software inventory, security tools, financial trails, biometrics, travel information, voting data, SSNs, and more.

It is built for advanced operators who can handle layered intelligence, target specific demographics, or need forensic-grade evidence.

Law enforcement, marketing teams, threat-prevention units, and researchers rely on this tier when they must map user behavior, track hardware fingerprints, or preserve digital evidence.

What is UMBRELLA CLOUD?

UMBRELLA CLOUD bundles company data, website databases, classified material, patents, trade secrets, customer and employee records, and timestamped telemetry.

This VIP tier is suited for intelligence agencies, data brokers, and high-end researchers who require both breadth and freshness.

[Live and NonLive datasets in both Basic and Full formats are included inside this tier.]
